Our Story

Sponge Pastry Shop is the creation of Chef Akantha. Having been a top pastry chef for several years in the hotel industry, his passion to bring forth the rich and tasteful delights to the public drew him to start up his own business. What started in 1985 as a small restaurant, “Sponge” is now an established and well-known name among everyone.

With a dedicated and friendly staff of over 80 men and women, we strive to provide the best quality food and service always. In keeping up with technological developments and consumer demand, we have been continually modernizing our processes and provide computer-based systems and online services.

Comprising of a skilled and artistic staff, we accept orders for wedding structures, design cakes, shaped cakes, and even customized & personalized sponge creations. Our menu currently consists of a diverse selection from sweets, cakes & savories to burgers, rice & noodles among many others.

Our dynamic catering team is equipped and ready to serve any occasion. With the flexibility to provide you with a customized menu, we ensure our service blends smoothly with any event.
